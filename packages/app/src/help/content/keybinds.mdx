## Cards (anywhere)

<Row keys="Left click">Add one copy to the active deck</Row>
<Row keys="Right click">Remove one copy</Row>
<Row keys="Ctrl + hover">Open the full card preview</Row>
<Row keys="Ctrl + scroll">Resize card tiles (shared across grid + deck)</Row>

## Cards in a deck

<Row keys="Alt + click">Move card between main and sideboard</Row>
<Row keys="Alt + Shift + click">Toggle commander on a legendary card — promote it, or release the current commander</Row>
<Row keys="Ctrl + right click">Set the card as the deck's cover art</Row>

## Cards in search results

<Row keys="Shift + click">Toggle pin (always shown, ignoring filters)</Row>
<Row keys="Shift + right click">Toggle favorite</Row>

## Navigation

<Row keys="T">Jump to the top (press again to go back)</Row>
<Row keys="D">Jump to the deck view (press again to go back)</Row>
<Row keys="A">Jump to analytics (press again to go back)</Row>
<Row keys="F">Jump to filters (press again to go back)</Row>
<Row keys="R">Jump to results (press again to go back)</Row>
